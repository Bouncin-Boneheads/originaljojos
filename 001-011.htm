"Jojo"
Bouncin' Boneheads Series 1, #011

Jojo himself, is of course a descendant of the original Jojos from the lost continent. He is not only the best friend to all the Jojos, he is considered the most skillful gamer. He bounces truer and tosses straighter than anyone else and should only be traded if you have a duplicate, and if you get at least 2 Jojos in return.
