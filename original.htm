The Original Jojo Game

  1. Determine who goes first with a bonehead toss-off. Flip a Jojo into the air and call heads or tails before it lands. Winner goes first.

  2. Take 6 Jojos and toss them into the air at the same time.

  3. Award points for:

    • Landing on feet ..... 6 Points
• Landing on side ..... 3 Points
• Landing on heads .... 1 Point
• Landing on tails .... 0 Points
  4. Play three rounds and tally the score.

  5. Winner gets his choice of 1 Jojo from each player.
