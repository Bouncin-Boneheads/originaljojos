The Jack Bones Game

  1. Line up 6 Jojo's about 1/2 inch apart. Increase distance according to skill.

  2. Toss a Jojo into the air and scoop up the first Jojo in line then catch the Jojo before it hits the ground.

  3. Put the Jojo back in line then try for 2 Jojo's before the catch.

  4. Keep going until you miss, then the next player trys and so on.

  5. The winner is the first who scoops up all 6 Jojo's and successfully makes the catch.
