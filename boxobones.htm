The Box O' Bones Game

  1. Find an empty egg crate or shoebox.

  2. Determine a tossing distance so that all players are behind the line.

  3. Start with an even number of Jojo's and a different color for each player.

  4. Each player, in turn, bounces his Jojo at least once trying to get it into the box. Experts try for two or more bounces.

  5. At the end of 10 throws, the player with the most Jojo's in the box wins.
