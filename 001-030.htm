"Bubba"
Bouncin' Boneheads Series 1, #030

Bubba is a bit of a clod. He always says the wrong thing and has no manners. Whenever one of the Jojos takes Bubba home for a play date, Bubba always gets in trouble or does the wrong thing. If you have Bubba in your collection, try to get him to be a better Jojo.
