"Chomper"
Bouncin' Boneheads Series 2, #045

Here's a JoJo with a real appetite for speed. Chomper or "Big Chomper" as he allows his friends to call him, is the heaviest of all JoJo's. When he's not stuffing his face, he's using his weight to crush through other JoJo's and plays a mean game of "Bouncin' off the Wall".
