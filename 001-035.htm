"Copperhead"
Bouncin' Boneheads Series 1, #035

Copperhead is a very special original Jojo. Even though he's got a head as hard as metal, he's quick and very sneaky. The other Jojos often appoint Copperhead as the lookout for each game. He always seems to know what the other players move will be. You will never be caught off-guard with Copperhead on your team.
