"Stinky"
Bouncin' Boneheads Series 1, #014

Stinky's best friend is Junkyard. Maybe because Junkyard smells so bad himself, he doesn't notice how much Stinky stinks. Stinky has a flat head and is really good at pushing other Jojos out of the way. If you get Stinky in your collection, try to keep him near Junkyard and away from the other Jojos
